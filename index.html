OUR CUSTOMERS

Our focus is firmly centered on serving the defense industry. That focus has helped our flagship product VBS3 grow to be the most widely used desktop training package among military organizations.

Through military user group meetings held annually at key industry events, our customers influence our product roadmap and present new training challenges for us to help them address. By staying close to our customers, we make sure their requirements stay at the center of all we do.

OUR TEAM

BISim’s staff is bohemian at heart. We’re a mix of adventurers, creatives, and unorthodox thinkers. Our team comes from military service, while others come from the video game industry, and still others bring years of business acumen from the broader software and technology industries. Together, we all share a common passion for making great games to train warfighters.
